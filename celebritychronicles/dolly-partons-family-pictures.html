Dolly Parton's Biography

Dolly Rebecca Parton was born on January 19, 1946, in Locust Ridge, Tennessee. She is the fourth of twelve children in a family that lived in a small cabin in the Great Smoky Mountains. Despite their financial struggles, Dolly's family instilled in her a strong work ethic and a deep love for music. These early influences played a significant role in her development as a singer-songwriter and entertainer.
